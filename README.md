# Discrete Dimensional Inheritance Model (DDIM)
**A Stochastic Resolution to Hubble Tension and Galactic Rotation**

### Abstract
The DDIM proposes that the universe is an emergent 3D Graft resulting from a sequence of discrete informational inheritance steps. By establishing a fixed **5.5% Probability of Finiteness**, the model identifies a natural phase transition into a stable physical state.

### 1,000-Run Simulation Results
* **Dimensional Stack Depth:** Converged on a global average mean of **19.14 dimensions**.
* **Hubble Expansion Shift:** Produced a global average expansion shift of **9.01%**.
* **Informational Viscosity:** Yielded a simulated viscosity of **0.3070 (30.7%)**.
